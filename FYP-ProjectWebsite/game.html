🚀 Release 20 June 2026

EverMotion v0.1 — Milestone 1 Build Now Available

Our first playable build is live! This release includes 6 mini-games across the Shoulder, Finger, and Wrist systems. Download the installer above and give it a try. Webcam required. Windows 10/11 only.

🛠️ Update 15 June 2026

Sky Reach Protocol & Micro Node Activation — Now Playable

Two flagship games are now fully implemented with MediaPipe hand tracking. Sky Reach Protocol targets shoulder rehabilitation through gesture-based energy node collection. Micro Node Activation targets finger precision. More games rolling out before final release in August.

📅 Event 13 June 2026

Mid-Point Demo Day — 13 June 2026

EchoCare presented EverMotion to our project supervisor and assessor today. The demo showcased our gesture tracking pipeline, game UI, and story mode flow. Thank you to Mr. Tian and Mr. Ee for the valuable feedback!
