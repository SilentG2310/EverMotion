Reflective Diary

Gavin's Reflection Journal

Week 1

12 April 2026 - 18 April 2026

Team Formation

Work Done

  • Reached out to group members through email and helped organize the team's first discussions.
  • Contributed to narrowing down the project direction and exploring possible project ideas.
  • Helped the team agree on developing a rehabilitation-based game.
  • Started working on the initial project proposal by reviewing requirements and expectations.

Leadership

  • Was appointed as the team leader after the team aligned on the project idea.
  • Took on responsibility for coordinating tasks and ensuring the team continued making progress.

Challenges

  • Managing different opinions within the group while trying to move forward efficiently was challenging.
  • Needed to balance listening to everyone with guiding the team towards a clear decision.

Reflection

  • This week helped me understand how structured and detailed the proposal needed to be.
  • I learned the importance of communication and decision-making as a leader during the early project stage.
Week 2

19 April 2026 - 25 April 2026

Research

Work Done

  • Focused on research and validation of the project idea.
  • Contacted and sourced rehabilitation centres and old folks homes to explore possible visits and insights.
  • Continued refining the project proposal with the team based on available research.
  • Explored suitable exercises for elderly users and how they could be translated into simple game mechanics.

Learning

  • Learned that accessing real-world users is not as straightforward as expected.
  • Developed a better understanding of user needs for designing a meaningful rehabilitation system.
  • Improved research skills through sourcing, validation, and adapting to limited access.

Challenges

  • Faced rejection when attempting to visit certain locations.
  • Had difficulty securing direct access to users, which limited primary data collection.

Reflection

  • I adapted by using online research and existing studies when initial plans did not work out.
  • This week showed me the importance of flexibility and problem-solving during research.
Week 3

26 April 2026 - 02 May 2026

System Planning

Work Done

  • Focused on translating project ideas into structured system components.
  • Developed user stories and worked on diagrams such as use case diagrams and system flows.
  • Continued refining the proposal by incorporating feedback and aligning sections with project requirements.
  • Reviewed and updated multiple parts of the document to improve clarity and consistency.

Learning

  • Learned to think from both a user perspective and a system perspective when designing software.
  • Improved my ability to structure ideas clearly before moving into actual development.

Challenges

  • Ensuring user stories, diagrams, and gameplay ideas were logically consistent was not straightforward.
  • Some ideas needed to be revisited and adjusted multiple times before they fit into a clear system structure.

Reflection

  • This week helped me understand how different features connect together within the system.
  • The planning work prepared the team for the transition from proposal writing to actual development.
